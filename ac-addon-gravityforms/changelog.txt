Release Date: October 6th, 2021

Release Date: September 21st, 2021

* [Improved] Add inactive forms as manageable tables

Release Date: June 25th, 2021

Release Date: June 18th, 2021

* [Added] UI feature like hide on screen implemented for Form Entry pages

* [Added] Editing for the Form Entry pages
* [Added] Smart Filtering for the Form Entry pages
* [Added] Column management for the Form Entry pages
* [Added] Initial release of the Gravity Forms integration, works with Admin Columns Pro version 5.5 and higher
